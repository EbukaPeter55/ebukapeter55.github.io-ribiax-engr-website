About Us

We are a cutting edge and
unique Engineering/construction
firm, known for its excellence and
innovation.

We have been continually involved in many projects for numerous clients. We have also provided engineering services for government
of all levels, private individuals and corporate organizations. We have also carried out plan and supervision
services for projects designed both in Nigeria and overseas.

During our years of continuous operation in Nigeria, our firm has made major contribution to all aspects of civil and structural engineering
development in the country and has acquired a database of local engineering conditions and methods of working thus enabling the
company to provide services geared towards meeting the specific needs of the clients.

Our practice is led by highly experience and qualified individuals in addition to some contract staff, which are registered with the Council for
the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN), which seeks to promote and regulate the education and
training of engineers in Nigeria and which provides an Engineer's code of conduct.

Organization

Great Importance is placed on individual and local responsibility, but
also on team collaboration. Each project is the responsibility of a
project team leader within a team. Every project team leader has
access to specialist skills within the firm, whether those skills are in the
office or elsewhere.

We work in multi-disciplinary teams to ensure co-oridnation between
disciples. We operate formal quality assurance systems, routinely
reviewing and auditing our work. We structure our project teams to
achieve clear lines of responsibility and communication with the client
and other consultants. By these measures, we add value to our clients
projects and achieve quality they can rely on.

International Working

As a foremost engineering consulting firm, REL is organized to enable
porject teams to be assembled across either disciplinary or
geographical boundaries in order to deliver the most appropriate
services to the client and her business needs. REL has also undertaken
various engineering services for contractors and investors.

The links between REL and its partners are enhanced by the REL
electronic network; the use of identical software and codes of
practice facilitates this working arrangement.
