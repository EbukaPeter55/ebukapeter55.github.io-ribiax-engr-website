Our Services

Engineering/Construction Services

Ribiax Engineering Limited has delivered a high level of expertise in a
number of related fields of engineering as follows:

Infrastructure Asset Management

This is an integrated multidisciplinary set of strategies in
sustaining public infrastructure assets such as water
treatment facilities, sewer lines, roads, utility grids, bridges
and railways, Generally, the process focuses on the later
stages of a faclity's life cycle, specifically maintenance,
rehabilitation and replacement. Asset management
specifically uses software tools to organize and implement
these strategies with the fundamental goal to preserve and
extend the service life of long-term infrastructure assets
which are vital underlying components in maintaining the
quality of life in society and efficiency in the economy. Our
expertise covers the complete spectrum of engineering
disciples as well as planning, management, environmental
consulting and infrastructural transaction advice.

Civil/Infrastructure

Projects in this category include infrastructure planning of
both urban and rural developments, Water/waste water
Engineering design and monitoring. Engineering design,
Public health design, Highways and bridges design. Rail, Civil
engineering design, infrastructural engineering design, Erosion
control and Land Reclamation works. Feasibility studies,
structural investigation/appraisals and strengthning of
existing structures e.g Bridges, Jetties, quays and sheet pile
walls.

Holistic Design Approach

During the initial phases of planning. It is key to work fast and be flexible, we
use a combination of sketching and advanced tools to define base
Infrastructure/structural and services concept - these can quickly be tested for
different solutions and enhance forward thinking.

We endeavour to explain our work graphically to enable
others to understand and contribute so as to achieve optimum solution.
