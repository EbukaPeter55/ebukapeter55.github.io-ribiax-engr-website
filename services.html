Ribiax Engineering Limited has delivered a high level of expertise in a

Infrastructure Asset Management

This is an integrated multidisciplinary set of strategies in
sustaining public infrastructure assets such as water
treatment facilities, sewer lines, roads, utility grids, bridges
and railways, Generally, the process focuses on the later
stages of a faclity's life cycle, specifically maintenance,
rehabilitation and replacement. Asset management
specifically uses software tools to organize and implement
these strategies with the fundamental goal to preserve and
extend the service life of long-term infrastructure assets
which are vital underlying components in maintaining the
quality of life in society and efficiency in the economy. Our
expertise covers the complete spectrum of engineering
disciples as well as planning, management, environmental
consulting and infrastructural transaction advice.

Sustainability

Ribiax considers sustainable development as a very critical
issue and have trained ourselves into developing a strategy
of clear and ambitious goals in order to design using the
natural capacity of our world. This goal is there to guide us
on how our building and infrastructure cann be:

  • Built using sustainable materials.
  • Able to cope with future climate change.
  • Sustainable maintenance and operation.
  • Carbon neutral.
  • Able to re-use material and be self-sufficient
